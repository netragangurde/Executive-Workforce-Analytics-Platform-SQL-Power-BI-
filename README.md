# Executive Workforce Analytics Platform

### SQL | Power BI | DAX | Workforce Analytics | HR Analytics | Business Intelligence

An end-to-end Executive Workforce Analytics Platform developed using SQL and Power BI to analyze workforce trends, employee attrition patterns, compensation metrics, and employee retention drivers across 1,000+ employee records.

The platform enables HR stakeholders to perform root-cause analysis of employee attrition and make data-driven workforce planning and employee retention decisions through interactive and executive-level dashboards.

---

## Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the most critical challenges faced by organizations. Understanding why employees leave, which workforce segments are at higher risk, and how compensation, employee satisfaction, and work-life balance influence retention is essential for strategic HR planning.

This project provides executive-level insights into workforce demographics, employee attrition, compensation trends, and employee retention drivers using advanced SQL analysis and interactive Power BI dashboards.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to answer questions such as:

- Which departments experience the highest employee attrition?
- Does overtime significantly impact employee turnover?
- How does compensation vary across different job roles?
- What relationship exists between employee tenure and salary?
- How do job satisfaction and work-life balance influence employee retention?
- Which employee segments require immediate HR intervention?
- How can HR leaders perform root-cause analysis of employee attrition?

This platform addresses these challenges by enabling data-driven workforce planning and employee retention analysis.
